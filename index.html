Building Your First PC

Have you ever considered building a PC? Whether it be for work, gaming, creative design, etc., building a PC is one of the most rewarding projects you could work on. This website is a step-by-step guide for someone with basic knowledge of computers!

From an outside perspective, building a computer can seem really complicated. Deciding what you want the PC to look like, choosing the perfect parts to suit your needs, actually putting the build together. There are many resources on the internet that can help you do this and this site is intended to walk you through each step of the process and provide resources to make your build go as smooth as possible!

Begin the process by navigating to the Picking the Parts page!
